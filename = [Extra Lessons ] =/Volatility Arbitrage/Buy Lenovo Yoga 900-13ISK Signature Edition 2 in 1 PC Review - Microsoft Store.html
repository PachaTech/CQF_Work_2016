    Overview

    Strike a pose

    From its innovative 360-degree watchband hinge to its eye-popping color options, the supercharged Lenovo Yoga 900 is anything but ordinary. This super-flexible 2 in 1 bends, flips, and folds into four different modes, and comes preloaded with 6th Gen Intel processing power and the customizable features of Windows 10. Combine that with improved battery life and you have a PC that’s ready for anything.

    Flex appeal

    This PC makes a great laptop, but that's just the tip of its flexibility. Fold it into a stand for a movie. Flip to a tent for games. Or lay it flat like a tablet.

    Brilliant QHD+ display

    With razor-sharp resolution of 3200 x 1800, images, movies, and games jump off the QHD+ screen with detailed realism and lifelike precision.

    Serious stability

    Made with 813 pieces of aluminum and steel, the durable watchband hinge won't wobble no matter how heavy-handed you get with the display.

    Perfectly portable

    Weighing in at just 2.8 pounds and only 0.59 inches thin, the Yoga 900 travels light and is a breeze to carry in a bag or from room to room.

    Cool factor

    The Yoga 900 features a re-engineered fan and specially designed vents in the hinge to keep everything cool, quiet, and dust-resistant.

    "The Lenovo Yoga 900 is like a Transformer wearing an Armani suit and a Rolex."
