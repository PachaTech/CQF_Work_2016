    Overview

    All-out performance

    Designed for working professionals, the thin and light VAIO Z laptop delivers performance without limitations. Its sleek style and sturdy build lend an artistic elegance, while Intel 6th Gen Core i7 processor provides lighting fast speeds and smoother multitasking. A stunning 13.3-inch WQHD touchscreen and Intel Iris Graphics deliver remarkable detail and rich color. Features like Windows 10 Pro, over 13 hours of battery life*, and a durable aluminum and UD carbon frame make this laptop hard to beat.

    Gaze deeply

    Everything looks razor sharp and lifelike with a WQHD display and texture-rich Intel Iris Graphics, which combine for pixel-perfect precision.

    Fast as lightning

    Get down to business in a flash with a speedy 256GB SSD that enables quick bootups and shut downs, and 8GB of memory for fast performance.

    Powerful Intel Core i7 processor

    Faster than ever, the 6th Gen Intel Core i7 delivers striking visuals, extended battery life, and higher data transfer rates.

    The quiet type

    The keyboard keys are finely tuned to sound softer than others, and also have a smudge-proof coating to keep them looking their best.

    Rough and ready

    The VAIO Z is built tough with an aluminum and UD carbon fiber frame, and a display made with impact-resistant tempered glass.

    Windows 10 Pro

    Get all the features of Windows 10 Home, plus important business functionality for encryption, remote log-in, creating virtual machines, and more.
